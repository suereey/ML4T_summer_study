# Q-Learning
## What is Q-learning
- Q is a table with 2 dimenssions, s (state) and a (action).
- How to use Q? 
    - pi(s) represents the policy take at state s.
    - The equation is: find the maximize Q[s,a]with different a value.
    - The * symbol means optimized value.
